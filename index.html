What We Do

Amazing features to report and complain
about accidents

Sri Lanka's Road Development Authority (RDA), in collaboration with the Sri Lankan Police and Insurance Companies, is proposing to develop a website to monitor traffic accidents, where drivers may report incidents and the RDA/Police can provide assistance.

The website is focused mainly with recording accidents, and drivers may use it to report incidents to the RDA (Road Development Authority), Insurance, and the Police. It is tough to contact insurance and police using standard procedures. This is a simple approach for us to apply this new technique website. Anyone may use this website to observe incidents that have happened all throughout the nation without reluctance.
See Accidents?

Report the accidents you see or meet with to us, we have the large network professionals, insurances and police officers working together to take care of you.

Report It

Our modern and robust technology infrastructure have made the complicated reporting accidents process to a single tap to your mobile on the palm of your hands

Take Care

While we protect and support you during trouble, we'd like to remind to follow the traffic rules and drive. For more care there are many insurance companies.

General Requirements when your connecting

There is a "Web Master" who create accounts for the Police, RDA, and Insurance Staff.

  • Drivers will register for free. Registered drivers can enter data (including photos), remove, and edit their own incidents.
  • The police and RDA will see the reported instances as a list, click to discover additional information, and then accept or reject them.
  • Police can also view the graphs, which include an analysis of the kinds of accidents, the reasons of the accidents, and the changes in accidents from time to time.
